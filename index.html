Issues We Repair

Your one-stop solution for all gadget repair needs

Display Changing

We replace cracked, unresponsive, or faulty screens on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and more. Our technicians ensure precise fitting and restore full touch and display clarity.

Networking

We diagnose and fix network and signal issues to improve connectivity, speed, and overall performance across your devices. From Wi-Fi dropouts to configuration problems, we ensure stable and seamless network access.

Software

We handle installation, troubleshooting, and updates of operating systems and applications. Whether it's fixing software errors, removing malware, or optimizing performance, we ensure your device runs smoothly.

Water Damage

We repair devices affected by liquid exposure that may cause corrosion, short circuits, or hardware failure. Our deep-cleaning and diagnostic process helps recover and restore functionality wherever possible.

Dead Phone Repairing

We specialize in reviving phones that no longer power on due to hardware faults, software crashes, or power supply issues. Our experts carefully diagnose and repair the root cause to bring your device back to life.

IC Repairing

We repair or replace damaged integrated circuits (ICs) on the motherboard that control key device functions. This service helps restore performance in cases of power failure, charging issues, or system instability.

Frequently asked questions

Which devices do you repair?

We repair a variety of devices such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktops, smartwatches, and gaming consoles.

What brands are covered by your repair services?

Our technicians are experienced with top brands including Apple, Samsung, Dell, HP, Lenovo, Microsoft, and many others.

Do your repairs come with a warranty?

Yes, we provide a 3-month warranty on all parts, guaranteeing the quality of our workmanship.

How can I send my device for repair?

You can visit our service center in person or arrange a pickup. Contact our support team to choose the best option for you.

Should I back up my data before repair?

Backing up your data is highly recommended before repair. While we handle devices with care, data loss can sometimes occur during repairs.

Repair durations depend on the issue complexity and device type. Minor repairs might be completed within hours, while major fixes may require several days.
